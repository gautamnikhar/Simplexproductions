Manufacturing B2B Global Brand

AVA HandFab — From 300 Followers to a Global B2B Brand

Despite being a premium hand-tufted rug manufacturer, AVA HandFab had just 300 Instagram followers and no brand identity that could connect with international B2B buyers. We changed that.

The Challenge

When AVA HandFab started with us, their Instagram page had just 300 followers and almost no content. Despite being a premium hand-tufted rug manufacturer, they had no brand identity that could connect with international B2B buyers.

Our Approach

We built AVA's branding around sustainability, women empowerment, and cultural heritage. Instead of selling rugs, we crafted a narrative that highlighted the artistry, the empowerment of local women, and the tradition behind every piece.

  • Launched consistent content series showcasing behind-the-scenes rug making, artisanship, and sustainability practices.
  • Highlighted AVA's cultural positioning at international trade shows.
  • Used storytelling content to emotionally connect with buyers instead of just showcasing products.

The Results

  • Followers grew from 300 to 4,000 in just six months.
  • Buyer inquiries increased from 4–5/month to 40–50/month on a contract basis.
  • At trade shows and showrooms, buyers were instantly impressed by the Instagram presence, using it as a trust-building portfolio.

Impact: AVA is now recognized as one of India's strongest B2B rug brands, competing globally with a story rooted in culture, tradition, and sustainability.
